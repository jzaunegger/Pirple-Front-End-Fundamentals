The Work of HP Lovecraft

About

HP Lovecraft was a horror fiction writer from the early 1900s. He was born August 20, 1890 in Providence, Rhode Island. He spend the majority of his life without fame for his writing and editing. He was not a prolific writer at the time, but the popularity of his work came after his death. He became popular for his mixiture of horrific elements and science fiction. He is most famous for such works as Call of Cthulhu, The Dream-Quest of Kadath, At the Mountains of Madness, The Shadow of Innsmouth, and The Shadow Out of Time.
